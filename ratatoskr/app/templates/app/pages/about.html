Why "ratatoskr"?

Before ratatoskr, during the COVID-19 pandemic, we were all forced online. Back then, we realized how unintuitive and outdated the tools we used were.
In 2022, we decided to remake the tools Worcester Tech used.

Alongside the redesign of techhigh.us, we created the ratatoskr meeting system: a modern meeting scheduling app made with teachers in mind.

It's also open source!
